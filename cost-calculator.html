How this is calculated

We keep the math simple and visible so you can trust it:

  • Ice cost/month = sessions per week × 4.33 weeks × (lbs of ice ÷ 10) × price per 10 lb bag.
  • Chiller electricity/month = (watts ÷ 1000) × hours per day × 30 days × your $/kWh rate.
  • Chiller filters/month = yearly filter cost ÷ 12.
  • Break-even = extra up-front chiller cost ÷ monthly saving vs ice.

These are estimates, not quotes. Real ice usage, chiller efficiency, and local prices vary. The goal is a realistic ballpark to compare the two paths — not an exact bill.
